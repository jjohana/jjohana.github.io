## Linking rules

Course subchapters are linked to QCMs only when all of the following are true:

- The question is active.
- The question has `qualityStatus = verified`.
- The question has the same `sectionId`, `topicId`, and `subtopicId` as the course subchapter.
- The question is not rejected or quarantined.
